Frequently Asked Questions

Are Cleanwave products safe for pets and children?

Yes, absolutely! All our products are formulated with non-toxic, plant-based ingredients that are completely safe for your little ones and furry friends when used as directed.

Are your bottles recyclable?

Yes, we are committed to sustainability. All our bottles are made from 100% recyclable PET plastic. We encourage you to recycle them after use.

Do your products contain harmful chemicals like parabens or sulfates?

No. Cleanwave products are free from parabens, sulfates, phosphates, ammonia, and other harsh chemicals commonly found in cleaners.

We are proud to be a "Make in India" brand. All our products are manufactured in state-of-the-art facilities in India, adhering to strict quality control standards.
